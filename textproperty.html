Iron Man

Iron Man is a superhero in american comic books published by marvel comics . The character was co-creater by writer and editor stan lee , developed by scripter larry lieber and designed bu artists Don Heck and jack kirby . The character made his first apperance in Tales of Suspense #39 (cover dated march 1963), and recived his own title in iron man #1 (may 1968) in 1963 the character founded the avenger superhero team with thor ant- man wasp wasp and the Hulk
